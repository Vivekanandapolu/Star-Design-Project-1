About Star

Star Design Institute is a renowned and prestigious independent institute in India that was founded in the year 2012. With a vision to provide world-class education in the field of design, the institute has established itself as one of the largest and most trusted institutions in the country.The institute offers a wide range of courses in various fields of design, including graphic design, fashion design, interior design, and product design, among others.The courses are designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of the subject matter, along with practical training and hands-on experience.

At Star Design Institute, the faculty is composed of experienced professionals and experts in their respective fields, who are committed to providing students with the best possible education and support. The institute boasts state-of-the-art facilities, including modern classrooms, design studios, and workshops, where students can hone their skills and creativity.

Over the years, the institute has produced numerous successful and talented designers who have made a mark in their respective fields. The institute's focus on providing a holistic education, along with its commitment to excellence, has earned it a reputation as one of the leading design institutes in India.

In conclusion, Star Design Institute is a highly esteemed and well-respected institute in India, known for its exceptional education and commitment to excellence in the field of design. Its contribution to the design industry in the country is unparalleled, and it continues to be a driving force for innovation and creativity.
