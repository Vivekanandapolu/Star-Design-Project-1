About this Programme

Our B.Des program in UX and Digital Product Design is a comprehensive four-year undergraduate program that equips students with the knowledge, skills, and practical experience necessary to excel in the rapidly evolving digital design landscape. Through a blend of theoretical coursework, hands-on projects, industry collaborations, and internships, students develop a deep understanding of design principles and emerging technologies, empowering them to create engaging and user-centric digital experiences.
