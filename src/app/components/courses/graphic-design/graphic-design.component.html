About this course

The programme is designed for individuals who wish to advance their careers in graphic design. This course opens up opportunities for learners in industries like advertising, publishing, digital media, eLearning or TV and films. Students will receive exposure to an actual work environment and acquire knowledge through direct observation. The Design Program prepares students for entry into the complex and interconnected world of professional design practice by providing an advanced academic structure for Graphic and Information Design studies, including studio practices,technics, histories and theories of design, and related topics.
